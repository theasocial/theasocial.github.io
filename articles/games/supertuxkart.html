SuperTuxKart

A racing game

Category games

If you like speed, challenge, and pretending to be a penguin—all at the same time—then SuperTuxKart is a game for you.

It’s a cartoonish, bright and colourful game, with minor in-race fighting, boosts, story mode, achievements, and other fun stuff. Apart from Tux, multiple other software mascots are available as playable characters, coming with their own carts that have different characteristics.

It’s a nice game to have around: even after passing the story mode and collecting all the achievements, it is fun to take a race or two once in a while.
